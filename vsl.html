Owning one specific corner of the yoga space is worth more than teaching yoga for everyone.

The teachers who break through — Debbie with back-pain relief yoga, Katrin with pre/postnatal yoga — didn't get lucky with a niche. They made a clear, deliberate positioning decision and then built everything on top of it. Direction Positioning is the first domino, and the one that makes the other two work faster.

Content that converts and an offer priced to actually sell — in that order.

Content Modeling replaces the exhausting "create something original every day" cycle with a smarter approach: find what's already working for positioned teachers in your space, make it yours, and build a following of people who want to buy, not just follow. Then price it where the math works: mid-ticket, $200–$500, where the conversion rate and the income potential intersect.
