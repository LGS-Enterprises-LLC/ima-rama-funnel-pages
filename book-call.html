What happens on the call

Here's the exact structure — so you know what to expect and how to prepare.

1

We start with your current situation

Your income right now, your audience size, your niche, what you're currently offering — or not offering. This isn't an interrogation. It's the baseline we need to give you anything useful. The more honest you are, the more valuable the call becomes.

2

We identify the one thing that's actually in your way

Not a list. Not ten things to optimize. The one constraint that, if you fixed it, would change everything else. For most yoga teachers, it's one of three things: unclear positioning, content that attracts followers but not buyers, or an offer that's priced wrong. We find out which one is yours.

3

We map the Hybrid Coaching system to your specific situation

Not a template. Not "here's what worked for someone else." A real look at how the three pillars — Positioning, Posting, Pricing — would apply to your niche, your audience, and where you're starting from. If Impact Academy is a fit, we'll explain why. If it isn't, we'll tell you that too.

✓

You leave with clarity — regardless of what you decide

The goal of this call is not to close you. It's to give you a clear answer to the question: "Is this the right next move for me?" If it is, great. If it isn't, you'll still walk away knowing more about your situation than when you arrived. That's a promise.
